About Us

Our founders bring together over three decades of experience in cybersecurity, AI, and finance. They have successfully founded and led AI companies and spearheaded cybersecurity engineering teams at some of the largest AI firms in the Bay Area. Additionally, they have extensive experience in venture capital, having spent many years investing in top cybersecurity and AI software technologies across Silicon Valley. United by a shared mission, they have come together to build Agamotto Security, leveraging their expertise in AI, technology, and finance to transform the Managed Security Service Provider (MSSP) landscape. Their goal is to empower MSSPs in the market with cutting-edge AI technology and strategic insights, driving innovation, efficiency, and enhanced security for all.
